SocialEyes Product Descriptions

eCommerce Product Copywriting and SEO Building

We were approached in July, 2016 by an online false eyelashes company who were trying to increase their search engine rankings. After analyzing their customer data (both Rich and Chanelle come from a background in economics - data analysis is child's play), they realized that the best thing for SocialEyes was a complete rewrite of all product descriptions and other writings. So that's what they did. 4000+ words later and SocialEyes is beginning to climb out of the dozens of Google pages to move to the top where it's owners want it.

  • Project: SocialEyes Copywriting
  • Client:SocialeEyes Lashes Ltd.
  • Category:Copywriting
  • Date:30/08/2016

With their data being analyzed using Tableau and the results being written using coffee, a Macbook and word, SocialEyes ended up with a new site of copy targeting keywords directly at their global target markets.
